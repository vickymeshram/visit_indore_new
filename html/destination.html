Take a Spiritual Trip to Ujjain

Want to take a one-day trip to connect with your spiritual side? Then Ujjain is your best bet while you are in Indore. Situated on the banks of the Kshipra River, this ancient city is a prominent Hindu pilgrimage center, thronged by worshipers from all over the world. You can seek the blessings of Lord Shiva at the famous Mahakaleshwar Temple, take a dip at Ram Ghat, enjoy a refreshing boat ride on the river, take a stroll around Sinhasan Battisi and Vikramaditya Temple or pay a visit to the Vedh Shala Observatory (Jantar Mantar).

Watch the Sunset at Gomatgiri

With 24 marble temples dedicated to the 24 Thirthankars, Gomatgiri is a sacred place for the Jain community. This small hilltop also has two other attractions – a huge 21-feet statue of Gomateshwar and the famous daal-baati served on Sundays inside the temple. Make sure that you visit the place during the evening hours to witness the surreal beauty of the setting sun while you unwind.

Trek to Janapav Hill

Located on Mumbai-Indore Highway, Janapav Hill is one of the favorite getaways of travelers in the Malwa forest region. You can either opt for a drive or trek your way to the top. Though it may be a tiresome task, the mesmerizing views from the hill make it all worthy. While you are here, unleash the nature enthusiast in you by going for forest exploration. You may be lucky to see a black buck or a brown fish owl. You can also visit Jamadagni Ashram dedicated to the Hindu sage Parashuram, who is believed to be born in these hill ranges.

Enjoy a Sumptuous Dinner at Chokhi Dhani

Chokhi Dhani is where you get to experience Rajasthan and its culture in the most authentic way. This village-themed resort serves the best of Rajasthani dishes, including the iconic Dal-Baati-Churma. That’s not all! You can also go for camel rides, attend folk art performances, and watch magic shows. Sounds like a perfect plan for a great evening, doesn’t it?

Explore Your Adventurous Side at Safari Activity Park

Transform your boring weekends into an adventurous one at this creatively designed activity park. With an exciting range of high and low rope activities, Safari Adventure Park is where you can plan a day out with your family. You can have fun at the splash pool, compete with your fellas at the Mud Run, find your balance at the artificial wall climbing or try your hand at tug of war. If you are not an adventure junkie, unwind in the serene environment with a variety of flowering plants, shrubs, and trees.

Explore Patalpani Waterfalls

This magnificent cascading marvel surrounded by dense forests is among the must-visit places on a trip to Indore. During monsoons, it flows down in its full glory, leaving tourists mesmerized. Descending from a height of 300 feet, this roaring, gurgling, and foamy waterfall creates a spectacular sight, to say the least. Entering or bathing in the fall is strictly prohibited due to the sudden flash floods. If you are looking for a one-day picnic spot near Indore, Patalpani Waterfalls is your best bet.
